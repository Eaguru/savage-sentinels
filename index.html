So who are the Sentinels?

The Savage Sentinels are a motley band of adventurers, spread across the multiverse. With humble roots in Varisia (the lovely town of Sandpoint, to be precise), the Sentinels have come a long way from simple beginnings.

From Faerun to Ravenloft, the Sentinels' influence and renown grows ever greater with each feat constantly topping our last. Our qualifications include, but are not limited to: slaying necromancers; unearthing dastardly cults; besting fiendish champions; sealing away primordial gods; slaying primordial gods; and making celestial beings bow to our will. This is not by any means an exhaustive list of credentials, and you are more than welcome to enquire about our full range of services.

We may not be the pristine, prestigious party of philanthropists you might have hoped for, and our methods may seem unorthodox—even reprehensible—we will always get the job done. Always.
